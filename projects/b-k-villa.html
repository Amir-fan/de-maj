B-K Villa

2022 • Jeddah, Saudi Arabia • Residential

The B-K Villa is a contemporary residential project that redefines luxury living through thoughtful design and attention to detail. The villa features a modern architectural language with clean lines and premium materials.

Designed for a family of five, the villa includes five bedrooms, a spacious living area, modern kitchen, and multiple outdoor spaces including a private garden and swimming pool area.

The design emphasizes natural ventilation, abundant natural light, and privacy while maintaining a strong connection to the surrounding landscape. The use of local materials and sustainable design principles ensures the villa is both environmentally conscious and culturally appropriate.
