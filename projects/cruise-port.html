Cruise Port Terminal

2024 • Dammam, Saudi Arabia • Transporting

This cruise port terminal project represents a significant investment in Saudi Arabia's tourism infrastructure. The terminal is designed to accommodate the largest cruise ships while providing passengers with a world-class arrival and departure experience.

The terminal features modern facilities including immigration and customs areas, retail spaces, restaurants, and passenger lounges. The design emphasizes efficiency, security, and passenger comfort while reflecting the cultural identity of the region.

Key design elements include a dramatic roof structure that provides shade and natural ventilation, clear wayfinding systems, and flexible spaces that can adapt to varying passenger volumes throughout the year.
