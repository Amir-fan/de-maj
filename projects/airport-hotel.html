Airport Hotel

2023 ‚Ä¢ Hotel - Leisure

This airport hotel project represents a perfect blend of functionality and aesthetic appeal, designed to serve the needs of both business and leisure travelers. The building features a contemporary design with clean lines and modern materials that create a welcoming atmosphere.

The hotel includes 200 guest rooms, conference facilities, restaurants, and recreational areas. The design emphasizes natural light, energy efficiency, and seamless connectivity to the airport terminal.

Key features include a dramatic glass facade that provides panoramic views of the airport operations, a rooftop terrace for relaxation, and state-of-the-art conference facilities that can accommodate both small meetings and large events.
