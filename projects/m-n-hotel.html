M-N Hotel

2024 • Riyadh, Saudi Arabia • Hotel - Leisure

The M-N Hotel represents the pinnacle of luxury hospitality design, featuring contemporary architecture and world-class amenities. The hotel is designed to cater to both business and leisure travelers seeking an exceptional experience.

Featuring 150 luxury suites and rooms, the hotel includes multiple dining venues, a spa and wellness center, conference facilities, and recreational amenities. The design emphasizes privacy, comfort, and personalized service.

Key design features include floor-to-ceiling windows with panoramic city views, premium materials and finishes throughout, and innovative lighting design that creates different moods throughout the day. The hotel's architecture makes a bold statement while providing a serene retreat from the bustling city.
